Traditional Bengali Recipes for coconuts

(My mum taught me these)

Living in the west, we most times are disconnected from cultural foods and, if you are unfortunate enough, return home after a long day to microwaved meals and the infamous beans on toast... Yikes

Myself however, a fellow coconutee, am here to save you from that dread no matter what situation you find yourself to be in. Whether you live with your family, or by yourself, or with a spouse - you can now enjoy these three bengali home-cooked meals and stink up the home with some good-old haldi, maris, zeera and daniya!

These meals should be relatively simple, and have been passed down to me through my mum who learnt from her mum who learnt from her mum who learnt from... so on and so forth. Feel free to adjust the spice level if you need but honestly these recipes aren't really that spicy.
